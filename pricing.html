Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and services

General Questions

What's included in the price?
All prices include design, development, mobile optimization, basic SEO, and contact forms. Premium plan includes a free domain for 1 year. Hosting is separate unless specified.
How long does it take to build my website?
Starter: 3-5 days, Basic: 5-7 days, Standard: 7-10 days, Premium: 10-15 days. Timeline starts after finalizing requirements and receiving content.
Do you offer hosting services?
Yes, I can help set up hosting for your website. I recommend reliable hosting providers and can manage it for you as part of the maintenance add-on.

Payment & Process

What's your payment process?
50% upfront payment to start the project, 25% after design approval, and 25% upon final delivery. All payments are via bank transfer, UPI, or digital wallets.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The upgrade cost will be the difference between your current plan and the new plan, plus any additional features.
Do you offer refunds?
Refunds are available within 7 days if no work has started. Once development begins, refunds are prorated based on work completed. See our full refund policy for details.
