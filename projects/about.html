Our Story

Established in 1995, Eternal Academy began as a small institution with just 150 students and a vision to transform education in the region. Today, we stand as one of the premier educational institutions in Jammu & Kashmir, with over 2,500 students and a legacy of academic excellence spanning more than two decades.
