Frequently Asked Questions

What is the selection process?

The selection process typically includes: 1) Application screening, 2) Written test (for teaching positions), 3) Demo class (for teachers), 4) Personal interview, 5) Reference check.

Is accommodation provided?

Yes, we provide subsidized accommodation for outstation faculty members. The accommodation includes basic furniture and utilities.

What is the work schedule?

Regular school hours are 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday to Saturday. Administrative staff may have different timings.

Do you hire freshers?

Yes, we do hire freshers for certain positions. We provide comprehensive training and mentorship for new joiners.
